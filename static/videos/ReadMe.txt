1. DESCRIPTION OF MULTIMEDIA CONTENT
-----------------------------------------------------------
This archive contains 7 video clips demonstrating real-world experiments on the dVRK platform as described in the paper.

File List:
1. Video_1_Needle_Sphere.mp4: Needle Pick with Sphere Obstacle. Demonstrates the RL policy picking a needle while the CBF enforces safety constraints around a spherical obstacle.
2. Video_2_Needle_Cylinder.mp4: Needle Pick with Cylinder Obstacle. Demonstrates needle picking with avoidance of a cylindrical obstacle.
3. Video_3_Gauze_Sphere.mp4: Gauze Retrieve with Sphere Obstacle. Shows the retrieval of surgical gauze avoiding a spherical obstacle.
4. Video_4_Gauze_Cylinder.mp4: Gauze Retrieve with Cylinder Obstacle. Shows the retrieval of surgical gauze avoiding a cylindrical obstacle.
5. Video_5_Suture.mp4: Multi-Stage Suturing (RL + CLF). Shows a complete suturing sequence, decomposing the task into RL-based grasping and CLF-based insertion.
6. Video_6_Lung_Top.mp4: Lung Tumor Resection - Top View (CLF + CBF). Top-down view of the resection task.(Playback at 5x real-time speed).
7. Video_7_Lung_Side.mp4: Lung Tumor Resection - Side View (CLF + CBF). Side view of the same resection task.(Playback at 5x real-time speed).
